## Compliance Requirements

### Space Dimensions
- Standard truck spot: min 18.0m × 3.5m
- Tractor only: min 8.0m × 3.5m
- Trailer only: min 14.0m × 3.5m

### Lane Widths
- One-way traffic: min 5.0m (recommended 6.0m)
- Two-way traffic: min 7.0m (recommended 8.0m)
- With parking maneuver: min 10.0m

### Safety Requirements
- Minimum 1m between vehicles
- Fire access lanes: min 4.5m width
- Emergency access: max 10m from any space
- Max dead-end length: 50m

### Turning Radius
- Standard truck: 12-13m
- Articulated vehicle: 12-14m
- Full combination: 14-18m
